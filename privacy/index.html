Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mosaicus Digital ("we", "our") handles information processed through the social media scheduling applications we operate, including those connected to the Pinterest API. Our scheduler is a self-hosted tool used to manage and publish content on social media business accounts owned or managed by Mosaicus Digital and its clients.

1. Information We Access

When a Pinterest account is connected to our scheduler, we access only the information required to schedule and publish pins on behalf of the account owner. This may include:

  • The Pinterest account profile (username, account ID).
  • Boards (names and IDs) so pins can be assigned to the correct board.
  • Pin content created through the scheduler (images, text, links, scheduled dates).
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens issued by Pinterest, used solely to authenticate API calls.

We do not collect Pinterest data beyond what is strictly necessary to operate the scheduling functionality.

2. How We Use the Information

Information accessed via the Pinterest API is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate the connected Pinterest account against the scheduler.
  • Create, schedule, and publish pins to boards owned or managed by the account holder.
  • Display connected boards within the scheduler user interface.

We do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise transfer Pinterest data to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

The scheduler is self-hosted on infrastructure controlled by Mosaicus Digital. All data, including OAuth tokens, is stored on our own servers and is not shared with external services. Tokens are kept in an encrypted database and used only to issue requests to the Pinterest API.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Information accessed via the Pinterest API is retained only for as long as the Pinterest account remains connected to the scheduler. The account holder can disconnect at any time, which triggers the removal of stored tokens and associated metadata from our database. Users may also revoke access at any time from their Pinterest account settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our scheduler is not directed to individuals under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
