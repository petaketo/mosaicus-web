Privacy Policy — Lucia Mora Scheduler

Last updated: 11 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the application Lucia Mora Scheduler ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when used in combination with the Pinterest API. The App is a self-hosted social media scheduling tool operated by Lucia Mora for the purpose of managing and publishing pin content on Pinterest business accounts owned or managed by the operator.

1. Information We Access

When you connect a Pinterest account to the App, we access only the information required to schedule and publish pins on your behalf. This may include:

  • Your Pinterest account profile (username, account ID).
  • Your boards (names and IDs) so pins can be assigned to the correct board.
  • Pin content you create through the App (images, text, links, scheduled dates).
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens issued by Pinterest, used solely to authenticate API calls.

We do not collect Pinterest data beyond what is strictly necessary to operate the scheduling functionality.

2. How We Use the Information

Information accessed via the Pinterest API is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate the operator's Pinterest account against the App.
  • Create, schedule, and publish pins to boards the operator owns or manages.
  • Display the operator's connected boards within the App's user interface.

We do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise transfer Pinterest data to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

The App is self-hosted. All data, including OAuth tokens, is stored on infrastructure controlled by the operator and is not shared with external services. Tokens are kept in an encrypted database and used only to issue requests to the Pinterest API.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Information accessed via the Pinterest API is retained only for as long as the Pinterest account remains connected to the App. The operator can disconnect their Pinterest account at any time, which triggers the removal of stored tokens and associated metadata from the App's database. Users may also revoke access at any time from their Pinterest account settings.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to individuals under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
