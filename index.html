Self-hosted tools for social media operators.

Mosaicus Digital builds and operates content scheduling and automation infrastructure for businesses managing presence across Pinterest, Instagram, and other social platforms.

What we do

  • Operate a self-hosted social media scheduler for our own and client accounts.
  • Build content pipelines that take a brand brief and produce scheduled posts on a calendar.
  • Run direct integrations with platform APIs (Pinterest, Meta/Instagram) under our own developer credentials.

Why self-hosted

Off-the-shelf social media schedulers either limit the number of brands an operator can manage or remove API access from their free tiers. Self-hosting lets us scale across multiple client accounts under a single operational footprint, and keeps content, scheduling data, and platform tokens on infrastructure we control end-to-end.
